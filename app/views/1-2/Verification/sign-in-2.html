Sign in to submit T Levels registration and results details

Awarding Organisations can use this service to:

  • register for T Levels they are providing
  • send student registration information
  • send student results for technical qualifications and check for missing results

Providers (schools and colleges) can use this service to:

  • confirm student industrial placements, maths and English, and any additional mandatory requirements
  • check for missing results
  • request certificates and statements of achievement
Start now

Before you start

Read through the detailed guidance for submit registration and results details for T Levels.

You must be an approved T Levels Awarding Organisation or Provider (school or college) to use this service. To find out more visit Introduction of T Levels.

You must have a Department for Education Sign-in account. Visit create an account if you do not already have an account.

The online service is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg).
