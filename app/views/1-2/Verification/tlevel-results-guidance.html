Guidance

Submit registration and results details for T Levels

Information for Awarding Organisations and Providers (schools and colleges) on how to submit registration and results details for T Levels.

Published 6 March 2018
Last updated 10 October 2019 — see all updates
From:
Education and Skills Funding Agency

T Levels are made up of a number of elements. In order for a student to receive their certificate, all results must be submitted before 31 July to a central system.

Awarding Organisation responsibilities

The Awarding Organisation must complete the registration process and submit grades before students can receive their qualification.

Registering for T Levels

Awarding Organisations must first complete the registration process by:

  • confirming the details of the T Levels their organisation will assess
  • adding schools and colleges to their T Levels and confirming their details
  • providing student registration details, including their course and school or college

Submitting grades

Awarding Organisations are responsible for providing the assessment results for the technical qualification, which include:

  • a grade for the core component, using A* to E
  • a separate grade for each occupational specialism, shown as pass, merit or distinction

The Awarding Organisation does not need to submit the overall T Level grade, as it will be worked out from the component and specialism grades.

Provider (school or college) responsibilities

The provider (school or college) must confirm that a student has completed the other components.

Registering students

Providers (schools and colleges) do not need to complete any registration with the Education and Skills Funding Agency (ESFA). They should use the existing registration process in place with the Awarding Organisation.

Confirming components

Providers (schools and colleges) are responsible for confirming that the student has:

  • met the minimum requirements for maths and English qualifications
  • completed the industry placement
  • met any additional mandatory requirements

How to submit registration and results details

Awarding Organisations and Providers (schools and colleges) will need to sign in to submit T Levels registration and results details to the Education and Skills Funding Agency (ESFA).

You can also choose to use your own systems to send registration and results details for T Levels instead. To find out how to make sure your system is compatible and allow it to send information directly, visit the T Levels developer hub.

You will need to have a Department for Education Sign-in Account. If you do not already have an account you will need to create an account.

All grades and components must be confirmed on or before 31 July for final grades to be issued in time for results day in August.

Requesting Certificates and Statements of Achievement

Once all results have been submitted, a student’s overall T Level grade will be calculated from the grades they achieved on the core component and the occupational specialism(s).

Students who do not pass all elements of their T Level can request a T Level statement of achievement which will show the elements they have completed.

Certificates and statements of achievement will be awarded by the Education and Skills Funding Agency (ESFA).

Providers (schools and colleges) can sign in to request certificates and statements of achievement .
